Frequently Asked Questions

How long is each therapy session? +

Standard therapy sessions are 15-45 minutes long. Initial consultations may take up to 45-60 minutes to ensure we thoroughly understand your needs and create an effective treatment plan.

How many sessions does it take to heal? +

Depends on the sevirty of condition, but usually 1-3 sessions are enough.

Are online sessions as effective as in-person? +

Research shows that online therapy can be equally effective for many conditions. The convenience and comfort of being in your own space often enhances the therapeutic experience.

What is your cancellation policy? +

I request at least 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Late cancellations may incur a fee. I understand that emergencies happen and will work with you on a case-by-case basis.
